📋 Phase Decisions — What is this?

After judges have scored all registrants for a phase, the organizing team reviews the Leaderboard and makes the official call on who moves forward. This page records those decisions.

1
Select the phase you are deciding on (e.g. “Phase 1 - Registration” to decide who advances to Phase 2).
2
Mark each registrant as Advanced, Eliminated, or leave as Pending.
3
The Leaderboard for the next phase will automatically show only registrants marked as Advanced here. The pool shrinks each round.

Judges can see decisions but cannot make them. Only the organizing team (Admin login) can advance or eliminate.
